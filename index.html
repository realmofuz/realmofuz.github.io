The Realm of Uz MMORPG

An exciting adventure across a vast, complex world built fully in
Minecraft. Defeat bosses, complete quests, and join up with your
friends to conquer many dungeons in an attempt to discover the deepest
secrets this world has to offer! Currently in alpha development.
Staff applications open.

Xyraith

Xyraith is an easy to learn, dynamically-typed, object-oriented programming
language designed for creating Minecraft servers. Currently in alpha
testing. If you want to help with the testing, simply join the discord
server, and ping Akarah.

Curse of Binding

An exciting souls-like RPG dungeon crawler map made in Minecraft, Curse of
Binding has 4 huge levels, 8 vicious bosses, over 20 unique weapon, most
of which have special abilities, custom combat mechanics, quests, an original
noteblock soundtrack, a leveling system, and much more added every update!
Currently in beta testing. To play, click the invite link to the server on the
"Other Projects" page of this website, and join the CoB discord server. From
there, you can download the map.

Our Ranks & Roles System

As a guild, we have agreed that rank and tier systems end
up doing more harm than good. However, we have a very basic
ranking system in our discord server, with the following:


Ranks

Member - At the bottom, you get this rank from joining the guild.


Active Member - The most common rank, giving you access to everything but
the most elite channels and permissions. You get this by being active in
our discord server.


Moderator - Only granted to the most trusted members of the guild,
moderators are tasked with, like the name suggests, moderating the guild
discord server, and punishing anyone who breaks our rules. Moderators have
access to all channels, and most permissions.


Owner - At the very top, only 2 people have this rank. Currently,
the 2 owners of our guild are Uzbuz and Nero, and very drastic changes would
be required to change that.


Roles

Our guild has a wide variety of roles, some of which can be chosen directly by
our members, while others will be granted to a player after they fulfill certain
requirements. If a member selects a role for themself, but they don't actually do
said thing, they will have that role taken away from them. Most roles do not grant
extra permissions. We have the following roles:


Self-given Roles

YouTuber - If you have a YouTube channel, no matter the size, choose this role.
Knowning that you have a channel will allow us to like and comment on your videos,
subscribe to you, promote your videos, and do collabs with you.


Artist - If you like making any form of art, choose this role. We love artists,
and sometimes we may ask you to make something for the guild.


Writer - If you like writing, choose this role. We occasionally host writing
contests, and additionally we may ask for your help on some of our guild projects.


Coder - If you like programming, choose this role. We will most likely ask for
your help on our various guild projects, as most of them require some form of programming.


Wynncraft Player - If you play Wynncraft, choose this role. Even though this guild
has transitioned away from being Wynncraft focused, we still have 2 threads on the forums,
and our ingame guild is still top 100. If you want to be invited to our ingame guild, message
or ping any member who has both the "Wynncraft player" role and the "OG" role.


Monumenta Player - If you play Monumenta, choose this role. Even though the
ingame UZB guild isn't very active, we occasionally do 8 person dungeon runs. If you
want to join the ingame guild, message or ping any member who has both the
"Monumenta Player" and "OG" roles.


Chat Revive - Choose this role if you want to revive conversations. If the
#general channel has little to no activity, and someone wants to revive it, they
might ping this role.


VC Ping - If you want to be pinged when someone joins VC, choose this role.
Before entering VC, people will usually ping this role.


Earnable Roles

UZB SMP player - Will be granted to one after said person joins the UZB SMP.
Note that if you break any rules on the SMP, you may be banned from the whole guild.
This role is currently unobtainable.


UZBRPG Content Maker - Will be granted to one after said person joins the UZBRPG
content team. Note that if you leak any info about the UZBRPG without permission, you
will likely be banned from the guild as a whole.


OG - The OG rank was granted to everyone who was ranked General or above before
we got rid of the ranking system. It is the only role with additional permissions, and
is no longer obtainable.


Custom Roles - These are granted to our active members who have contributed a good
amount to the guild. There are also people who gained a custom role due to their rank in
the former system. These are purely cosmetic.
