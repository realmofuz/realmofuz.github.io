Xyraith

What it is

Xyraith is an easy to learn, dynamically-typed, object-oriented programming
language designed for creating Minecraft servers. Designed by Endistic, Xyraith
is made with Kotlin, and will be used to program the UZBMMORPG. For more information,
click the link on the home page to join the Xyraith discord server.
