Our Ranks & Roles System

As a guild, we have agreed that rank and tier systems end
up doing more harm than good. However, we have a very basic
ranking system in our discord server, with the following ranks:


Newbie - At the bottom, you get this rank by just joining the guild.

Member - The most common rank, giving you access to everything but
the most elite channels and permissions. You get this by being active in
our discord server.


Moderator - Only granted to the most trusted members of the guild,
moderators are tasked with, like the name suggests, moderating the guild
discord server, and punishing anyone who breaks our rule. Moderators have
access to all channels, and most permissions.


Owner - At the very top, only 2 people have this rank. Currently,
the 2 owners of our guild are Uzbuz and Nero, and very drastic changes would
be required to change that.
