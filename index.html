Our Ranking System

Contribution and Activity Systems

Minor Contribution to the guild = making some sort of small media (art/short vid)
relating to it/helping someone with a uzb project/making an event etc
Major Contribution = making a longer video/forum thread/adding a major thing to
a uzb project/making a major event such as an ARG/boosting the server, major
contributions count as 3 minor ones
Activity = joining conversations, starting conversations, joining vc

Ranks and Requirements

Recruit (Acolyte) - Base rank

Recruiter (Priesthood) - Fairly frequent activity (once per week min) for a month or 1 contribution

Captain (Clergy) - Somewhat frequent activity (several times per week min) for a month or 3 contributions

Strategist (Inquisitor) - Frequent activity (4 times per week min) for a month or 5 contributions

General (Magus) - Very frequent activity (once per day) for a month or 8 contributions, General will be voted in by the existing generals and chiefs

Chief (Baron) - Chiefs will be promoted by the existing chiefs and owner
